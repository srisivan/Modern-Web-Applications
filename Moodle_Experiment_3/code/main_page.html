About Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. Widely referred to as the "Missile Man of India," he played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development.

Awards and Honors:

Bharat Ratna (1997)

Padma Vibhushan (1990)

Padma Bhushan (1981)

Achievements

Development of SLV-III

Pokhran-II Nuclear Tests

People's President of India
